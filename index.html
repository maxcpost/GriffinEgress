    Frequently Asked Questions

    If you cant find an answer to your question? Give us a call!

    • Do I need a permit for an egress window?

      Yes! You absolutly need a permit for any sort of egress construction. Our team will point you in the right direction to get started!

    • Can I install an egress window in a finished basement?

      Yes, you can! Most of our installations are on either finished basements or basements in the finishing process.

    • Will the small excavator cause a bunch of damage to my yard?

      No way! We have an entire process to make sure your lawn stays as you left it.

    • How long does it take to install?

      1 DAY and we're done. We implement unique and proprietary techniques to get the job done faster, cheaper, and better than our competitors.

    • What areas do you serve?

      We serve the entire greater Rochester area. Brockport, Hilton, Spencerport, Henrietta, and Irondequoit are just a few of the outstanding communities we proudly serve.
